> GNU **nano**, a text editor for Unix-like systems

**scroll to the end**: `Alt`+`/`

**copy**: `Alt`+`6`

**cut**: `Ctrl`+`k`

**paste**: `Ctrl`+`u`

copy-cut-paste applies to the current line or to the selection

**select**: `Alt`+`m`+`a`
